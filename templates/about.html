Our Story

The journey of SubTranscribe from idea to reality

2022: The Beginning

SubTranscribe started as a small project to help content creators add subtitles to their videos more easily.

2023: Growing the Team

As demand grew, we expanded our team and improved our AI models for better accuracy and language support.

2024: Platform Expansion

We launched our cloud platform, allowing users to access their transcriptions from anywhere and collaborate with team members.

2025: Today and Beyond

Today, SubTranscribe serves thousands of users worldwide, from individual content creators to large media companies.
